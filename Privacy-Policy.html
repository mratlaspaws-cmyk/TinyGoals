Privacy Policy for TinyWins

This Privacy Policy describes how TinyWins ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our goal tracking mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Goal Data (Stored Locally Only)

Your Goals Stay Private: All your goal information is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to our servers.

Information you create that stays on your device includes:

  • Goal Details: Goal names, descriptions, durations, and start times
  • Progress Data: Time elapsed, completion percentages, and status
  • Statistics: Success rates, streaks, completion history
  • Money Tracking: Optional cost calculations and savings estimates
  • Settings: App preferences, currency selection, notification settings
  • Goal History: Past goals and their outcomes

1.2 Device Permissions (When You Grant Them)

TinyWins may request the following permissions to function properly:

Permission Purpose Required
POST_NOTIFICATIONS Send progress updates and motivational messages Optional
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM Precise timing for milestone notifications Optional
WAKE_LOCK Keep notifications active when device sleeps Optional
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Restart notifications after device reboot Optional
INTERNET Display advertisements (AdMob) Required
ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE Check internet connectivity for ads Required

Important: You can control all these permissions through your device settings. The app will continue to work with limited functionality if certain permissions are denied.

1.3 Technical Information (Not Personally Identifiable)

We may collect limited technical information to improve the app:

  • App version and device model (for compatibility)
  • Crash reports (only if you choose to send them)
  • Basic usage analytics (through AdMob, see section 8)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Local Processing Only

Your goal data is used exclusively on your device to:

  • Track your goal progress and display statistics
  • Send local notifications and reminders
  • Calculate completion percentages and streaks
  • Display motivational messages based on your progress
  • Maintain your goal history and preferences

2.2 Service Improvement

Technical information may be used to:

  • Fix bugs and improve app performance
  • Ensure compatibility with different devices
  • Optimize user experience and features

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage Architecture

No Cloud Storage: TinyWins stores all your data locally using Android's secure storage systems.

Your data is stored using:

  • SharedPreferences: App settings and basic preferences
  • Local Files: Goal data and history (JSON format)
  • Notification State: Temporary data for managing alerts

3.2 Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • Android's built-in app sandboxing (data isolated from other apps)
  • Device-level encryption (if enabled on your device)
  • No network transmission of personal goal data
  • Standard Android file permissions
Important: Since data is stored locally, you are responsible for backing up your goals if desired. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will permanently delete your goal history.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 No Personal Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal goal data to any third parties. This is because:

  • All goal data remains on your device
  • We have no access to your goal content or progress
  • No personal information is collected by our servers

4.2 Third-Party Integrations

TinyWins integrates with the following services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (see section 8)
  • Android System Services: For notifications and alarms

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 Complete Control

Since all data is stored locally, you have full control:

  • Access: All your data is accessible through the app interface
  • Modification: Edit or delete goals and data at any time
  • Export: No built-in export, but data remains on your device
  • Deletion: Clear all data by uninstalling the app or using "Clear All Data"

5.2 Notification Controls

You can control notifications through:

  • Android notification settings for TinyWins
  • App-specific notification preferences
  • Do Not Disturb mode on your device

6. Data Retention

6.1 Local Data Retention

Your goal data is retained locally until:

  • You manually delete specific goals or use "Clear All Data"
  • You uninstall the TinyWins app
  • You clear app data through Android settings
  • Your device is reset or replaced

6.2 No Server-Side Retention

Since we don't collect your goal data, there is no server-side retention period. Your privacy is protected by design.

7. Children's Privacy

TinyWins is not specifically designed for children under 13, but it doesn't collect personal information regardless of user age. If a child uses the app:

  • All data remains local to their device
  • No personal information is transmitted to us
  • Parents can review local data through the app interface
  • AdMob may apply additional restrictions for child users (see section 8.5)

8. Advertising Services (Google AdMob)

8.1 What is AdMob

TinyWins displays advertisements through Google AdMob to keep the app free. AdMob is a third-party advertising service that may collect data independently from TinyWins.

8.2 Information Collected by AdMob

Note: This data collection is performed by Google AdMob, not by TinyWins directly.

AdMob may collect:

  • Device Identifiers: Google Advertising ID, Android ID
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, screen size
  • Network Information: IP address, mobile carrier, network type
  • App Information: App name, version, and usage patterns
  • Ad Interaction Data: Clicks, views, impressions, and engagement
  • Approximate Location: Based on IP address or location services (if enabled)

8.3 How AdMob Uses This Information

Google uses AdMob data to:

  • Display relevant advertisements to users
  • Measure ad performance and effectiveness
  • Prevent advertising fraud and abuse
  • Provide aggregate analytics to app developers
  • Improve their advertising services

8.4 Your Advertising Choices

You can control ad personalization:

Android Devices:

  1. Go to Settings > Google > Ads
  2. Toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
  3. Tap "Reset advertising ID" to get a new anonymous ID

Google Ad Settings:

  • Visit Google Ad Settings
  • Manage your ad preferences and interests
  • Opt out of personalized advertising

8.5 Children and Advertising

Google AdMob has specific protections for children under 13 and may:

  • Disable personalized advertising for child users
  • Show only family-appropriate advertisements
  • Apply additional data protection measures

9. International Users

9.1 Global Availability

TinyWins is available globally, and since data is stored locally, your information stays in your country/region on your device.

9.2 Currency Support

The app supports multiple currencies for money tracking, with preferences stored locally on your device.

10. Data Breaches

10.1 Minimal Risk

Since TinyWins stores data locally and doesn't maintain user databases, the risk of data breaches affecting your personal goal information is minimal.

10.2 Notification Procedure

In the unlikely event of a security issue affecting the app:

  • We will investigate and assess the scope
  • Users will be notified through app updates
  • Recommended protective actions will be provided
  • Relevant authorities will be notified if required

11. Third-Party Links and Services

TinyWins may link to or integrate with external services:

  • Email Apps: When sharing crash reports or feedback
  • App Stores: For updates and reviews
  • Support Websites: For help and documentation

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

12.1 Update Notifications

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect:

  • Changes in app functionality
  • Updates to legal requirements
  • Improvements to privacy practices

12.2 How We Notify You

Material changes will be communicated through:

  • In-app notifications
  • App update descriptions
  • Updated "Last modified" date on this policy

Continued use of TinyWins after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

13. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Union, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Legitimate Interest: Providing core app functionality
  • Consent: For optional features like notifications
  • Contract: To provide the service you've requested

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or TinyWins' privacy practices:

14.1 Contact Information

  • Email: help.mr.atlas.paws@outlook.com
  • In-App: Use the feedback feature in Settings

14.2 Response Time

We aim to respond to privacy inquiries within:

  • General questions: 48 hours
  • GDPR requests: 30 days
  • Urgent privacy concerns: 24 hours

Summary: TinyWins is built with privacy by design. Your goals, progress, and personal data stay on your device. We only interact with advertising services to keep the app free, and you have full control over your information at all times.
